About Us

We are an Austrian company
that has been engaged in the production
of sliding chests for over 70 years.
With our production facility in Bad Wimsbach,
we guarantee "MADE IN AUSTRIA".
For years, we have been striving to improve perfection and quality
and further develop automation.
Our experience in manufacturing quality products
is always a guarantee for making the right choice.
In addition to our sliding chests,
we also produce well shaft covers and snow shovels.
In recent years, we have continuously evolved
our focus on the construction sector.
Whether in the area of concrete mixers,
scaffolding, or transport equipment,
we offer a wide selection of products that meet all requirements.
Our expertise and the extensive variety in the
construction accessories sector will also convince you.
We, Altrad Hofmaninger, are a team that works hand in hand.
This makes us confident that we will continue to be an important
and reliable partner for our business partners in the future.
