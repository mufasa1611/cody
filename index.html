About Us


We are an Austrian company that has been engaged in the production of wheelbarrows for over 70 years. With our production facility in Bad Wimsbach, we guarantee "MADE IN AUSTRIA" quality, and we are always striving to further improve the perfection and the quality of our products.

In addition to our wheelbarrows, we also produce well shaft covers and snow shovels. Also, in recent years, we have continuously evolved our focus on the construction sector. Whether in the area of concrete mixers, scaffolding or transport equipment, we offer a wide range of products that meet all industry standards and requirements. Our expertise and our extensive variety in the construction accessories sector will convince you.

We, Altrad Hofmaninger, are a team that works hand in hand. This makes us confident, that we will continue to be an important and reliable partner for our business partners and customers in the future.
